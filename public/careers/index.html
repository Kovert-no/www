Do you find it exciting to find security holes in web applications? Or maybe move around in a corporate IT network without being detected? Do you want to work in an environment that puts quality first? Do you want to have dedicated time to study and develop your offensive safety skills?

About the position

As a penetration tester in Kovert, you are part of a small, inclusive and highly competent team that carries out many different assignments for our customers. The assignments vary in both scope and length, and contain everything from testing web applications, targeted phishing attacks or deep dives into the customer's network, as well as counceling with customers. On our list of customers, we have both small, medium and large players who offer challenging and interesting days at work.

We are looking for someone

  • with good communication skills both orally and in writing, in Norwegian and English.
  • with knowledge of, or experience with security testing of
    • web applications
    • internal and external networks / infrastructure
    • source code
    • social engineering
    • hardware and IoT
    • physical security mechanisms
  • who found it tedious to do things more than once, so you see joy in automating repetitive tasks.
  • with experience as a penetration tester.
  • who have relevant education, courses or certifations.
  • who can get security clearance

We offer

  • Varied and exciting assignments for customers in a number of different industries and sectors.
  • The ability to influence your work day and work tasks
  • Flexible working hours
  • Continuous learning and skill building
  • 25% of your time dedicated to research and self-development (courses, conferences and certifications)
  • Competitive terms and conditions
  • Opportunity to participate in the company's future option program
  • A great social environment
